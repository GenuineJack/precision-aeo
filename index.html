    Answer Engine Optimization (AEO) is the practice of optimizing your content to be easily understood and cited by AI‚Äëpowered search engines like Claude, Perplexity, and Google's AI features. Unlike traditional SEO, AEO focuses on structured data, clear answers, and authoritative signals that help AI systems extract and present your information accurately.

    Our tool performs comprehensive analysis including page speed testing via Google PageSpeed Insights API, content structure analysis, healthcare compliance checking, and technical SEO auditing. We evaluate six core factors: Content Structure (25%), Authority Signals (20%), Technical Optimization (20%), Content Clarity (15%), YMYL Compliance (10%), and User Experience (10%).

    Healthcare content is classified as YMYL (Your Money or Your Life), requiring higher standards of authority and accuracy. With clinicians and patients increasingly using AI assistants for medical information, proper AEO ensures your content is accurately cited with appropriate medical disclaimers, author credentials, and safety information.
